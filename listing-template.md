## Deduplication Checklist

When you find the same property on multiple sites:

1. **Identify duplicates by:**
   - Same parcel ID/APN
   - Exact same acreage + location
   - Same photos across sites

2. **Keep the best listing:**
   - Most detailed description
   - Most photos
   - Most recent updates
   - Best contact info

3. **Note all URLs:**
   - List all sources where it appears
   - Might have different prices or terms

## Regional Quick Reference

### Portland OR Metro (PDX)
**Counties:** Washington, Clackamas, Yamhill, Columbia (NOT Multnomah)
**Distance check:** Use Google Maps to verify < 1hr to PDX

### WA Columbia Gorge (PDX)
**County:** Skamania (up to Stevenson)
**Distance check:** Must be < 1hr to PDX, focus on Stevenson area

### Tacoma WA Area (SEA-TAC)
**Counties:** Pierce, south King County
**Distance check:** < 1hr to SEA-TAC, emphasize Pierce over King

### Santa Fe NM Area (ABQ)
**Counties:** Santa Fe, Bernalillo, Sandoval
**Distance check:** < 1hr to ABQ Sunport

### Southern California Border (SAN)
**County:** San Diego
**Distance check:** < 1hr to San Diego International, focus on border area

### Colorado Front Range (DEN/COS)
**Counties:** El Paso, Douglas, Jefferson, Boulder, Larimer
**Airports:** Denver Int'l (DEN) or Colorado Springs (COS)
**Distance check:** Flexible - either airport works

## Zoning Red Flags to Watch For

**AVOID these terms:**
- "Agricultural use only"
- "No residential structures"
- "Recreational property"
- "Conservation easement"
- "Grazing rights only"
- "Timber harvest restrictions"
- "No permanent structures"

**GOOD terms:**
- "Residential zoning"
- "Build-ready"
- "No restrictions"
- "Unrestricted"
- "Single-family permitted"
- "Rural residential"

---

## Time-Saving Tips

1. **Use browser tabs:** Open 5-10 listings at once, skim for red flags
2. **Filter fast:** Eliminate obvious "no's" quickly (wrong location, bad zoning)
3. **Save maybes:** Bookmark anything promising for deeper review
4. **Batch research:** Look up all promising properties on county assessor sites at once
5. **Screenshot key info:** Easier than copying/pasting everything
